Heading to the airport…

Posted by backpackeracker on January 10, 2009

My backpack is loaded once again and I’m leaving for the airport in a few hours.  Will spend the night at a distant family member’s house in Bangkok.  Will grab the overnight train from Bangkok Monday night and arrive ready for the three day trek through the jungle on Tuesday morning in Chiang Mai.

Here is a map of Thailand so you can see where exactly I’ll be.  Chiang Mai first, then east to Cambodia for a week, then south to Phuket where we will catch boats and island hop for a few weeks.

On a side note, my last blog post commented on National Pride and how many Asians have this deep, deep national pride.  Well, I think this story only hammers my point home:

This story comes from China, here are some bits of it.

“For the third time, he’s tasted the flesh of an unwitting intruder in his pen at the Beijing Zoo.
His most recent victim was 28-year-old Zhang Jiao, who told CNN he fell into the panda pen Wednesday while trying to catch a small toy thrown by his young son.   (this is where it gets good)

“The panda is a national treasure, and I love and respect [him], so I didn’t fight back,” Zhang said. “The panda didn’t let go until it chewed up my leg and its mouth was dripping with my blood.” 

So there you have it.  Imagine the self-restraint and what he was thinking…”Golly, there’s a giant 280 pound panda chewing on my thigh bone, I should totally kick it off of me—no wait—it’s a national treasure, I’m gonna let this one play itself out.”

I found that story amusing.  Here is the full article courtesy of CNN.

I will post an entry Thursday night from an internet cafe in Chiang Mai after my trek.  That is, if all goes according to plan, which it usually never does on a trip…

Thanks for all your comments, talk to you all from Chiang Mai.

peace
